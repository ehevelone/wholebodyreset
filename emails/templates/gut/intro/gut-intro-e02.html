As gut support begins, it can be helpful to know what actually matters during this stage.

Progress here is rarely dramatic. Instead, it often shows up as subtle shifts in tolerance and predictability.

Over time, you might notice:

• fewer digestive surprises
• less urgency or reactivity around meals
• slightly steadier energy after eating

You may also notice very little at first. That doesn’t mean nothing is happening.

Early gut support is often about calming and stabilizing before anything feels obviously “better.”

There’s nothing to analyze or change right now. Just notice patterns gently as they emerge.

We’ll continue forward at a pace your body can tolerate.
