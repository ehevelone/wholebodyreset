One of the most valuable skills in maintenance is recognizing when nothing needs to change.

If your days feel predictable, your energy feels manageable, and your body isn’t asking for adjustment, that’s information worth honoring.

Many people leave maintenance too early because it doesn’t feel like “doing” something.

But staying steady is often what allows deeper resilience to take hold over time.

You’re not missing a step. You’re not stuck.

You’re maintaining what your body trusts.
