One of the most reassuring signs during maintenance is when nothing feels urgent.

No strong reactions. No pressure to adjust. No sense that something needs fixing.

This doesn’t mean progress has stopped. It often means your body has enough support to operate without constant compensation.

Many people mistake calm for stagnation. In reality, calm is often the environment where resilience quietly deepens.

If things feel uneventful right now, that’s not something to solve.

It’s something to protect.
