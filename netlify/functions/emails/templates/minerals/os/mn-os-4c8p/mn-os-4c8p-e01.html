If mineral support has made you feel more sensitive, overstimulated, or uncomfortable, we want you to hear this clearly:

Nothing has gone wrong.

Minerals can shift nervous system signaling, fluid balance, and cellular activity — even when doses are modest.

For sensitive systems, that can feel like too much, not because minerals are harmful, but because capacity is limited right now.

In these moments, the most supportive response is to slow down — not push forward.

You do not need to fix this. Pausing or reducing is information, not failure.
