Nervous System — Regulation & Pacing

The nervous system determines how much change the body can tolerate. When regulation is strained, even supportive inputs may feel overwhelming.

Stability, predictability, and pacing are prerequisites for deeper work.

If symptoms escalate quickly or recovery feels delayed, slowing down is often the most supportive response.
