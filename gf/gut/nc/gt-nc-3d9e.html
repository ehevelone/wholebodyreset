No Change Is Still Feedback

This page is part of the Whole Body Reset — Guided Foundations program. There is nothing to implement here. Observation alone is sufficient.

The absence of change is not failure. It is information.

You may want to notice:

  • whether hydration and minerals feel easier to tolerate
  • whether reactions feel less intense or shorter-lived
  • whether digestion feels less “on edge” overall

These are signs of readiness forming, even when symptoms have not shifted yet.

Progress often begins as quiet cooperation.
