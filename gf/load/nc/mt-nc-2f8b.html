What Quiet HMD Looks Like

  • symptoms are mild or improving
  • you don’t feel like you’re “detoxing”

That’s success — not failure.

Quiet HMD is not “doing nothing.” It is building exit capacity.

What Aggressive Detox Looks Like

Aggressive approaches tend to force movement before the body can safely handle it.

Common signs the body is being pushed:

  • new or worsening anxiety
  • insomnia or racing thoughts
  • head pressure / headaches
  • skin flares
  • constipation or digestive shutdown
  • crashes after “detox days”

These are not “proof it’s working.” They are signs the load exceeded capacity.
