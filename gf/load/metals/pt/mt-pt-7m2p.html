Heavy Metal Detox Protocols

This chapter provides educational examples of how metal detox protocols are commonly structured. These are not requirements, prescriptions, or recommendations. Many participants do not implement metal protocols during Guided Foundations.

Core Principle

Cilantro mobilizes metals.
Chlorella binds metals.
They are never used separately.

Beginner Protocol (Educational Example)

  • Morning: fresh cilantro or 1 tsp dried
  • Wait 30 minutes
  • Chlorella 1–2 grams
  • Hydration throughout the day
  • Afternoon: gentle metal-support tea

Intermediate Protocol (Educational Example)

This level is not appropriate for sensitive systems, active neurological symptoms, kidney instability, or concurrent pathogen detox without practitioner guidance.

  • AM: ginger + turmeric + black pepper tea
  • 20 minutes later: cilantro
  • 30 minutes later: chlorella (3–5 g)
  • PM: zeolite or bentonite clay
  • Cycle: 5 days on / 2 days off

When Metal Detox Is Not Appropriate

  • Active anxiety, panic, or insomnia
  • Poor bowel regularity
  • Kidney or bile flow instability
  • Recent reactions to detox or binders

Metal protocols require stability first. Skipping foundations often creates setbacks.

Reduce Re-Exposure

  • Glass or stainless cookware
  • Filtered water
  • Aluminum-free deodorant
  • Avoid aerosols and synthetic fragrances

Metals leave slowly — and that is a good thing.
