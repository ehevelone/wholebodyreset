When Detox Feels Like Too Much

During parasite-focused work, the body may release more ammonia, metals, or inflammatory byproducts than it can comfortably clear at once.

When this happens, symptoms may increase — not because something is wrong, but because clearance systems are temporarily overloaded.

Common signs of overload may include:

  • head pressure or headaches
  • fatigue or heaviness
  • increased anxiety or restlessness
  • digestive changes or appetite shifts

These experiences are not a sign to push harder. They are signals that the pace exceeds current capacity.

In Foundations, the most stabilizing response is usually to reduce pressure — not to optimize detox.

Slower pacing often leads to steadier regulation and more sustainable progress.
