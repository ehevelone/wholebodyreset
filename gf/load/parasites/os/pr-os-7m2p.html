Your Body Is Speaking — Let’s Listen

Feeling worse does not mean something is wrong. In most cases, it means the body released more than it could comfortably process at this time.

When load exceeds capacity, symptoms are often the body’s way of asking for a slower pace — not a different strategy.

Some common experiences during this phase include:

  • headaches or a sense of internal pressure
  • fatigue, heaviness, or slowed energy
  • increased anxiety or restlessness
  • temporary changes in stools or appetite

These responses are not failures. They are pacing signals.

The body is communicating that it needs more time, more steadiness, or less stimulation in order to regain balance.

This is a pause for regulation — not a stop, and not a setback.
