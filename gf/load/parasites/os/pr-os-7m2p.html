Your Body Is Speaking — Let’s Listen

Feeling worse does not mean something is wrong. It usually means the body released more than it could comfortably process.

Some people notice:

  • headaches or pressure
  • fatigue or heaviness
  • increased anxiety or restlessness
  • changes in stools or appetite

These are not failures — they are feedback. The body is asking for slower pacing.

This is a pause, not a stop.
