When Extra Support Makes Sense

If symptoms continue even after the pace has been reduced, it may simply indicate that the body would benefit from additional perspective or support.

Some people choose to consult:

  • a clinical herbalist
  • a functional or naturopathic practitioner
  • a practitioner experienced in terrain-based approaches

This does not mean something has gone wrong. It reflects that different systems sometimes require different layers of support.

Choosing guidance is not a departure from Foundations. It is simply another way of creating safety and clarity when the body is asking for it.

Education builds understanding. Support builds safety. Both are valid.
