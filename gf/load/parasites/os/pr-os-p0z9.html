When Extra Support Makes Sense

If symptoms continue even after slowing the pace, it may be a sign that additional support would be helpful.

Some people choose to consult:

  • a clinical herbalist
  • a functional or naturopathic practitioner
  • a practitioner trained in terrain-based detox approaches

This does not indicate failure. It simply reflects that your body may need a different level or style of guidance.

Education builds confidence. Support builds safety.
