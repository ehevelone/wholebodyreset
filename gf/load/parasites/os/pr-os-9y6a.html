Staying Regulated Matters More Than Speed

Detox only works when the nervous system stays regulated. Overwhelm pushes the body into protection, not healing.

Signs regulation needs support:

  • panic or racing thoughts
  • insomnia or vivid nightmares
  • heart pounding
  • digestive shutdown

These signals invite grounding, nourishment, and rest — not more cleansing.

Stabilization is progress.
