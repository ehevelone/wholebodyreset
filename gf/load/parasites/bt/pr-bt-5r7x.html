When the Body Responds Well

Improvement is information — not an instruction to rush.

If you’re noticing consistent improvement, it often means parasite load is decreasing and toxin handling has stabilized.

Common signs at this stage:

  • More consistent energy
  • Better digestion after meals
  • Reduced anxiety or irritability
  • Improved sleep patterns
  • Less reactivity to herbs

This does not mean parasites are “gone.” It means the terrain is improving.

Terrain strength always comes before eradication.

Stay with the current cycle. Avoid adding new herbs or increasing doses. Stability now prevents setbacks later.
