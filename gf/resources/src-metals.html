What “Metals” Refer To

In environmental, industrial, and biological literature, the term “metals” refers to a broad category of naturally occurring elements. Certain metals are discussed in health, ecological, and occupational contexts due to their persistence and interaction with biological systems.

Metals in Terrain-Based Discussions

Terrain-focused and systems-based models often examine metals as one of many environmental variables that may influence physiological balance over time. This perspective appears in historical, ecological, and integrative frameworks rather than reductionist models.
