What Black Walnut Hull Is

Black walnut hull refers to the outer green husk of the black walnut tree (Juglans nigra). It has a long history of use in traditional herbal, agricultural, and ethnobotanical contexts and is widely discussed in botanical and historical literature.

Quality & Sourcing Considerations

  • Clear identification of species (Juglans nigra)
  • Proper drying and storage to prevent mold or degradation
  • No added preservatives, dyes, or flavoring agents
  • Transparent harvesting, processing, and origin disclosures

Reputable Supplier Categories

Black walnut hull is commonly sourced through botanical suppliers, herb farms, and bulk raw-material distributors. When evaluating sources, preference is often given to suppliers that provide origin transparency and batch testing documentation.
