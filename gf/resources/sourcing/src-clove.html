What Clove Is

Clove refers to the dried flower buds of the clove tree (Syzygium aromaticum). It has a long history of use in culinary, agricultural, and traditional herbal contexts and is widely discussed in botanical and historical literature.

Quality & Sourcing Considerations

  • Clear identification of plant species and part used
  • Freshness and proper drying to preserve aromatic compounds
  • No added fillers, dyes, or artificial flavoring
  • Transparent origin, harvesting, and processing disclosures

Reputable Supplier Categories

Clove is commonly sourced through botanical suppliers, spice merchants, and bulk raw-material distributors. In educational and industry contexts, suppliers are often evaluated based on freshness, origin transparency, and handling practices.
