What Wormwood Is

Wormwood most commonly refers to Artemisia absinthium, a bitter aromatic herb within the Artemisia genus. It has a long history of reference in botanical, agricultural, and ethnobotanical literature and is widely discussed in historical herbal texts.

Quality & Sourcing Considerations

Educational and industry discussions commonly emphasize:

  • Clear identification of species (Artemisia absinthium)
  • Verification of plant part used (leaf and flowering tops)
  • Proper drying and storage to preserve aromatic compounds
  • No added fillers, dyes, or undisclosed ingredients
  • Transparent origin, harvesting, and processing disclosures
