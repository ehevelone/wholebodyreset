What Bentonite Clay Is

Bentonite clay is a naturally occurring clay derived from volcanic ash, composed primarily of montmorillonite minerals. It is widely discussed in industrial, cosmetic, agricultural, and traditional health literature due to its absorbent properties.

Quality & Sourcing Considerations

  • Clear labeling regarding intended grade (cosmetic, industrial, or food-grade)
  • Independent third-party testing for heavy metals and contaminants
  • No added fragrances, flavors, dyes, or fillers
  • Transparent sourcing, mining location, and processing disclosures
