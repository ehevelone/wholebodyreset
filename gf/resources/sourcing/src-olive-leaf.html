What Olive Leaf Is

Olive leaf refers to the leaves of the olive tree (Olea europaea). It has a long history of use in traditional, agricultural, and botanical contexts and is frequently discussed in historical and scientific literature related to the olive plant.

Quality & Sourcing Considerations

  • Clear identification of plant species and leaf material
  • Transparent harvesting and drying practices
  • Disclosure of extraction or processing methods when applicable
  • No added fillers, dyes, or undisclosed ingredients

Reputable Supplier Categories

Olive leaf is commonly sourced through botanical suppliers, herb farms, and bulk raw-material distributors. In educational and industry contexts, suppliers are often evaluated based on origin transparency, handling practices, and quality documentation.
