What Olive Leaf Is

Olive leaf refers to the leaves of the olive tree (Olea europaea). It has a long history of use in traditional, agricultural, and botanical contexts and is widely discussed in historical and scientific literature related to the olive plant.

Quality & Sourcing Considerations

Educational and industry discussions commonly emphasize:

  • Clear identification of plant species and leaf material
  • Transparent harvesting and drying practices
  • Disclosure of extraction or processing methods when applicable
  • No added fillers, dyes, or undisclosed ingredients
