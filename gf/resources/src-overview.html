Sourcing & Ingredient Education — Overview

This page provides orientation for the Sources section of Whole Body Reset – Guided Foundations. The materials here are educational references only and are not instructional, prescriptive, or protocol-based.

Why This Section Exists

Many substances referenced in health and wellness discussions are mentioned without clear explanation of what they are, where they come from, or what quality considerations matter. This Sources section exists to provide clear, neutral context for those materials.

Each Sources page is designed to explain a substance or concept in plain language, outline general sourcing considerations, and identify commonly referenced supplier categories.

How to Use the Sources Pages

These pages are intended to function as reference material. Readers may consult them as needed for background understanding when a substance or concept is mentioned elsewhere in the program. There is no expectation to apply, follow, or implement anything described in this section.
