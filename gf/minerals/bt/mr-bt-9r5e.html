Stability Is a Win

Not all improvement feels exciting.

With minerals, progress often shows up as fewer swings rather than a sudden surge of energy.

Stabilizing signs may include:

  • More predictable sleep
  • Less anxiety or nervous system tension
  • Reduced cravings
  • Fewer daytime “crashes”

These shifts suggest balance is improving.

As stability increases, the body is quietly building capacity.

Capacity matters more than intensity.
