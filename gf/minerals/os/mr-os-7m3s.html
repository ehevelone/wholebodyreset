When Balance Tips Too Far

Mineral balance is dynamic. Shifts can sometimes feel noticeable as the body recalibrates toward equilibrium.

Some people become aware of:

  • changes in digestion
  • feelings of heaviness or low energy
  • mental fog or reduced clarity
  • a general sense of being “off”

These experiences are informational — not indicators of harm or failure.

Balance often restores itself as the system settles and integrates change.

The body tends to correct gently when given time and steadiness.
