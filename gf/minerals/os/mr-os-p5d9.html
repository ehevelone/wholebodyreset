Your Nervous System May Need a Break

Mineral shifts can sometimes be felt through the nervous system before they register elsewhere.

Increased sensitivity, reactivity, or internal agitation does not mean something is wrong. It often reflects a system that is still integrating change.

Regulation tends to return as the nervous system settles and regains a sense of safety.

Calm is not something to force — it emerges when the system is ready.

In Foundations, safety and regulation always come before momentum.
