Your Body Is Adjusting

Mineral changes can bring temporary sensations before the body settles into balance.

Some people notice physical or nervous system responses as mineral levels shift and regulation recalibrates.

Common adjustment signals may include:

  • heightened awareness of heartbeat or circulation
  • feelings of alertness, restlessness, or jitteriness
  • muscle tension, twitching, or tightness
  • changes in digestion or appetite

These experiences are informational — not signs of harm or failure.

The most supportive response here is patience. Regulation happens as the body finds a new equilibrium.

Allowing time and steadiness often brings greater comfort than pushing for change.
