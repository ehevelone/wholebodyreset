Sometimes Change Is Quiet

Mineral shifts don’t always register as clear improvement or decline. Sometimes they simply feel different.

People may notice:

  • subtle changes in sleep rhythm
  • shifts in appetite or cravings
  • periods of calm followed by mild fatigue
  • energy that fluctuates rather than spikes

These are not problems. They are signs the system is reorganizing.

Awareness comes first. Adjustment only happens if and when it’s needed.
