Binders — No Immediate Change

Not noticing anything yet is very common at this stage.

Binders often work quietly in the background. Their role is prevention — not sensation.

The absence of strong reactions can mean toxins are being caught before they cause symptoms.

Gentle Reflection

  • Do symptoms feel less chaotic overall?
  • Are things at least not getting worse?
