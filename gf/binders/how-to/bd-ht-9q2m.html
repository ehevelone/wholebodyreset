Binders — How to Start Safely

This page provides clear starting guidance. Nothing here requires escalation. The goal is protection, not detox intensity.

When to Start Binders

Begin binders only after hydration is consistent and bowel movements are regular.

If bowel movements are not daily, address that first before starting binders.

Starting Amounts (Choose One Binder)

  • Chlorella: 250–500 mg once daily
  • Bentonite Clay: ½ teaspoon in water once daily
  • Zeolite (liquid or powder): lowest labeled dose once daily

Start with one binder only. More is not better at this stage.

Timing Rules (Critical)

  • Take binders at least 1 hour before food, or
  • 2 hours after food
  • Never take binders with medications or supplements

Binders do not distinguish between toxins and nutrients. Timing protects what your body needs.

What to Expect in the First Days

  • No noticeable change
  • Mild improvement in clarity or steadiness
  • No increase in symptoms

The absence of symptoms is a positive response.

How Long to Stay Here

Remain at this starting level for 3–5 days before considering any change.

If things feel calm and stable, continue exactly as is.

Calm is not a pause in progress. It is evidence that your body is no longer fighting the process.

When binders are tolerated without reactions, it means toxins are being captured and removed without overwhelming circulation or the nervous system.

Changing doses during this phase often creates problems that were not there before. Stability allows clearance to continue quietly, without triggering new symptoms.

In Guided Foundations, uneventful days are a sign that the terrain is finally holding.
