Binders — When the Body Feels Overloaded

Overload does not mean harm. It means the pace may be too fast.

Detox is not a race. The nervous system must feel safe for progress to continue.

Supportive Adjustments

  • Reduce binder amount
  • Take binders less frequently
  • Increase hydration
  • Pause other detox steps

Gentle adjustments often resolve symptoms quickly.
