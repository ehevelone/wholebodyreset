Binders — Listening to Body Feedback

Your body communicates through sensations, not alarms.

Some people notice:
• constipation
• dryness
• heaviness
• tightness

What These Signals Mean

These often suggest:

  • The binder dose may be too strong
  • Hydration may need adjustment
  • Elimination pathways need support

This is about refinement — not failure.
