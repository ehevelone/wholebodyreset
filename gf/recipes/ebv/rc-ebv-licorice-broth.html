Licorice Root Adrenal Broth

A nourishing, grounding broth for EBV-related fatigue and adrenal depletion.

Why This Works

Licorice root supports cortisol balance and adrenal resilience — two systems heavily impacted during chronic EBV activity.

  • Supports adrenal hormone balance
  • Reduces EBV-related exhaustion
  • Calms inflammation
  • Supports thyroid-adrenal communication

Recipe

  • 2 cups bone broth (or vegetable broth)
  • 1 tsp licorice root (cut or powder)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • Pinch black pepper

Preparation

Simmer gently for 10–15 minutes. Strain and sip warm.

When to Use

  • Midday or early evening
  • During EBV fatigue phases
  • After stressful days or poor sleep

Avoid daily long-term use if blood pressure is elevated. Rotate as needed.
