Licorice Root Adrenal Broth

This broth is shared as an optional, nourishing support within Guided Foundations. It is commonly used during periods of fatigue, stress recovery, or EBV-related depletion.

Why Licorice Root Is Used

Licorice root has traditionally been used to support adrenal resilience and cortisol balance. These systems are often under strain during chronic stress or immune challenges.

  • Supports adrenal and stress response balance
  • May reduce fatigue associated with immune strain
  • Supports inflammatory regulation
  • Helps stabilize thyroid–adrenal communication

Broth Ingredients

  • 2 cups bone broth or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon licorice root (cut or powder)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric
  • Pinch of black pepper

Preparation

Simmer gently for 10–15 minutes. Strain and sip warm.

When to Use

  • Midday or early evening
  • During EBV-related fatigue phases
  • After periods of stress or poor sleep

Licorice root is not intended for continuous daily use. Avoid or rotate if blood pressure is elevated, and always follow individual tolerance.
