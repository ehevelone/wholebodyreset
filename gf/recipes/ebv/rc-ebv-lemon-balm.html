Lemon Balm Calming Tea

One of the most important herbs for EBV, thyroid irritation, and nervous system calming.

Why This Works

Lemon balm helps suppress viral activity while calming the nervous system. It is especially helpful for EBV-related throat tightness, anxiety, and sleep disruption.

  • Calms EBV-driven inflammation
  • Soothes thyroid irritation
  • Reduces adrenaline spikes
  • Supports sleep and emotional regulation

Recipe

  • 1 tbsp dried lemon balm
  • 1 tsp chamomile (optional)
  • ½ tsp ginger (optional)
  • 8–12 oz hot water

Preparation

Steep 10 minutes, covered. Strain and sip slowly.

Best Time to Use

  • Evening or before bed
  • During EBV flares
  • When throat, neck, or thyroid feels irritated

Safe for regular use and ideal during metal or parasite work.
