Gentle Parasite Support Tea

This tea provides low-intensity parasite support while prioritizing digestion, nervous system calm, and detox tolerance.

This is not a parasite cleanse. It is a regulation-focused option used when stronger herbs are not tolerated or not yet appropriate.

Why a Gentle Approach Matters

Parasite activity is closely tied to inflammation, gut motility, bile flow, and nervous system tone. For many people, calming and regulating these systems reduces parasite burden indirectly.

This tea is especially useful:

  • before starting tinctures
  • during breaks from stronger parasite herbs
  • when anxiety or overstimulation is present
  • during maintenance phases

Ingredients

  • Clove — mild antiparasitic activity; supports balance
  • Ginger — improves digestion and bile flow
  • Peppermint — reduces gas, bloating, and cramping
  • Thyme — gentle antimicrobial and gut support

Preparation

  • Use ½–1 tsp total herb blend
  • Steep in hot water for 10 minutes
  • Strain and sip warm

Start with smaller amounts if sensitive.

When to Use

  • Between parasite cycles
  • When tinctures feel too strong
  • With meals to support digestion
  • During nervous system flare days

This tea pairs well with hydration, binders, and lymph support.
