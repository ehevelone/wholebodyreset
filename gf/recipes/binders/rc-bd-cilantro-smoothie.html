Cilantro Support Smoothie

This recipe is shared as a gentle, food-based option sometimes used during metal-focused support phases or long-term maintenance.

There is no requirement to use this recipe. Read and apply only what feels supportive.

Ingredients

  • 1 packed cup fresh cilantro
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup pineapple (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup filtered water or coconut water
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon spirulina (if tolerated)

Preparation

Blend all ingredients until smooth. Consume fresh.

Why these ingredients are used:

  • Cilantro is traditionally used for gentle metal support
  • Pineapple supports digestion and lymph movement
  • Banana provides potassium for nervous system balance
  • Spirulina may offer mild binding support for some people

Effects vary by individual. Gentle responses are preferred over intensity.

When Some People Choose to Use This

  • During periods of metal-focused support
  • Occasionally during maintenance phases
  • After parasite or binder cycles
  • Following sweating or sauna sessions

This recipe is intentionally mild. If discomfort occurs, reducing frequency or pausing is appropriate.
