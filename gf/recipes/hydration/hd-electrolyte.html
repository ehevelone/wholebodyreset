Electrolyte Mineral Tea

This tea uses prepared Pink Himalayan sole water to provide gentle, food-based mineral support without synthetic additives or loose salt.

Using sole water allows minerals to enter the body in a controlled, biologically usable way without overstimulating the nervous system.

Ingredients

  • Warm filtered water
  • Prepared Pink Himalayan sole water
  • Juice of ½ fresh lemon
  • 1 teaspoon raw honey (optional)

Preparation

Fill your mug with warm (not hot) filtered water. Add ½–1 teaspoon of prepared sole water per cup. Stir gently, then add lemon and honey if desired.

When to Use

  • Morning hydration
  • After sweating or sauna
  • Low-energy afternoons
  • During binder or detox phases

Why This Helps

  • Supports mineral replenishment
  • Helps stabilize adrenal output
  • May reduce dehydration-related headaches
  • Supports nervous system regulation

If the tea feels stimulating, reduce the amount of sole water or return temporarily to plain hydration water.
