Electrolyte Mineral Tea

This recipe provides gentle, food-based electrolyte and mineral support without synthetic additives. It is commonly used throughout Guided Foundations as a daily hydration option.

This tea is designed to support hydration and mineral balance without overstimulating the nervous system.

Ingredients

  • Warm filtered water
  • Juice of ½ fresh lemon
  • 1 teaspoon raw honey
  • Pinch of pink Himalayan salt

Preparation

Combine all ingredients in warm (not hot) water. Stir gently until dissolved.

When to Use

  • Morning hydration
  • After sweating or sauna
  • Low-energy afternoons
  • During detox or binder phases

Why This Helps

  • Supports mineral replenishment
  • Helps stabilize adrenal output
  • May reduce dehydration-related headaches
  • Supports nervous system regulation

If symptoms feel stimulating, reduce salt or honey amounts and return to plain hydration water.
