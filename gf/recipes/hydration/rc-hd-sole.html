Proper hydration allows minerals to enter cells, lymph to move, binders to work, and detox pathways to stay open.

What Sole Water Is (and Is Not)

Sole water is a concentrated mineral brine made from Pink Himalayan salt and water.

  • It is not meant to be consumed directly
  • It is not electrolyte powder
  • It is not a detox drink

It is a preparation step that allows hydration to actually work.

Step 1 — Make the Sole Concentrate

Use a glass jar or glass cup only. Do not use plastic or metal.

  • Add Pink Himalayan salt (¼–½ cup, depending on crystal size)
  • Fill with clean, filtered water
  • Stir briefly
  • Cover and let sit at room temperature

When the water becomes mostly clear and salt remains at the bottom, the solution is fully saturated.

⚠️ Do NOT drink the concentrate directly

Drinking sole water straight can cause aggressive flushing, nausea, dizziness, or electrolyte imbalance.

Step 2 — How to Use Sole Water Daily

Add the concentrate to your daily drinking water.

  • Use 2–4 tablespoons per gallon of water
  • Start at the lower end if sensitive
  • Sip throughout the day as normal water

This turns plain water into mineralized hydration that supports cellular function instead of diluting minerals.
