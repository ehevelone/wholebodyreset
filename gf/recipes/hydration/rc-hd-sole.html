Pink Himalayan Sole Water

This is the foundational hydration method used throughout Guided Foundations. It replaces adding loose salt to water and allows minerals to be absorbed in a controlled, biologically usable way.

Proper hydration allows minerals to enter cells, lymph to move, binders to work, and detox pathways to stay open.

What Sole Water Is (and Is Not)

Sole water is a concentrated mineral brine made from Pink Himalayan salt and water.

  • It is not consumed on its own
  • It is not electrolyte powder
  • It is not a detox drink

It is a preparation step that makes hydration effective instead of diluting.

Step 1 — Make the Sole Concentrate

Use a glass jar or glass cup only. Do not use plastic or metal.

  • Add Pink Himalayan salt (¼–½ cup, depending on crystal size)
  • Fill with clean, filtered water
  • Stir briefly
  • Cover and let sit at room temperature

When the water becomes mostly clear and salt remains at the bottom, the solution is fully saturated.

⚠ Do NOT drink the concentrate directly

Drinking sole water straight can cause severe diarrhea, nausea, dizziness, or electrolyte imbalance.

Step 2 — How to Use Sole Water Daily

Add the concentrate to your daily drinking water.

  • Use 2–4 tablespoons per gallon of water
  • Start at the lower end if sensitive
  • Sip throughout the day as normal water

This turns plain water into mineralized hydration without overstimulating the nervous system.

Why This Works

  • Supports lymph thinning and movement
  • Improves mineral uptake
  • Reduces headaches and pressure
  • Supports kidney filtration
  • Stabilizes nervous system signaling
  • Improves tolerance to binders and detox

When to Adjust

Reduce the amount slightly if you experience:

  • Lightheadedness
  • Overstimulation
  • Loose stools

Hydration should feel supportive — not forceful.
