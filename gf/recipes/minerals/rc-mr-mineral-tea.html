Mineral Rebuilder Tea

A nourishing herbal infusion designed to gently restore minerals, calm the nervous system, and support recovery after detox or stress.

This tea focuses on rebuilding, not detoxing. It is ideal after periods of depletion.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon nettle leaf
  • 1 tablespoon red raspberry leaf
  • 1 tablespoon lemon balm

Preparation

Add herbs to hot water and steep for 10–15 minutes. Strain and sip warm.

What This Supports

  • Mineral replenishment
  • Nervous system calming
  • Lymphatic flow and recovery
  • Post-detox stabilization

When to Use

  • After parasite or metal work
  • During periods of fatigue or depletion
  • In the evening for nervous system support
  • During long-term rebuilding phases

This tea is gentle enough for regular use. If overstimulation occurs, reduce lemon balm slightly.
