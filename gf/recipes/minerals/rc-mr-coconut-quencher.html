Coconut Mineral Quencher

A light, food-based hydration option used after sweating, heat exposure, or increased lymph movement.

This is not a daily hydration replacement. It is a situational mineral recovery drink.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz coconut water (unsweetened)
  • 8 oz filtered water
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • Pinch of sea salt or Pink Himalayan salt

How to Use

Mix ingredients together and sip slowly. Best consumed shortly after sweating or heat exposure.

Why This Helps

  • Replenishes potassium and sodium
  • Prevents post-detox or post-sweat crashes
  • Supports adrenal and nervous system recovery
  • Improves hydration tolerance

When to Use

  • After sauna or sweating
  • After outdoor heat exposure
  • Following lymphatic movement or exercise
  • During periods of fatigue or dehydration

If bloating or blood sugar swings occur, reduce coconut water and increase plain mineralized water.
