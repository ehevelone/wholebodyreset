Brazil Nut Selenium Boost

A simple, food-based way to support selenium intake during immune, thyroid, and detox support phases.

This is a food-based recipe, not a supplement protocol. Selenium is provided in its natural form as found in whole foods.

Ingredients

  • 1–2 whole Brazil nuts

How to Use

  • Eat alone as a snack
  • Add to a smoothie
  • Chop and sprinkle over oatmeal or yogurt

Why This Helps

  • Provides natural selenium for antioxidant support
  • Supports immune regulation
  • Protects cells during detox and oxidative stress
  • Supports thyroid resilience without overstimulation

More is not better. Small, consistent amounts are sufficient.

This recipe pairs well with hydration, mineral teas, and gentle detox phases.
