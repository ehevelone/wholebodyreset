Dandelion Root Liver Support Tea

A gentle, food-based liver and bile support tea used during detox phases, parasite work, metal support, and maintenance.

This tea does not force detox. It supports liver drainage so toxins can exit without congestion or pressure.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon dried dandelion root
  • 8–12 oz filtered water

Preparation

Simmer gently for 10 minutes. Strain and sip warm.

Why This Helps

  • Supports bile flow
  • Improves hormone and toxin clearance
  • Reduces liver congestion during detox
  • Supports digestion and stool regularity

When to Use

  • During parasite or metal detox phases
  • When experiencing sluggish digestion
  • Alongside binders and hydration
  • During maintenance for gentle liver support

Start with small amounts. Liver support should feel relieving — not activating.
