Nervous System Calming Shot

A fast-acting nervous system support used during anxiety spikes, overstimulation, insomnia, or detox-related adrenaline surges.

This is not a sedative and not a detox. It is a regulatory tool that helps the body shift out of fight-or-flight.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz warm water
  • ½ tsp magnesium glycinate or magnesium chloride
  • ½ tsp lemon balm tincture (or strong tea)
  • ¼–½ tsp prepared Pink Himalayan sole water
  • Optional: ¼ tsp raw honey

How to Use

  • Mix ingredients thoroughly
  • Sip slowly or take as a small shot
  • May be repeated after 30–60 minutes if needed

When This Is Helpful

  • Panic or anxiety waves
  • Racing thoughts or heart palpitations
  • After parasite or metal dosing
  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • “Wired but exhausted” feeling

This shot pairs safely with binders and hydration and does not interfere with detox pathways.
