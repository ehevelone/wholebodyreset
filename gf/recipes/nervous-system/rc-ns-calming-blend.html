Nervous System Calming Herbal Blend

This blend helps shift the body into parasympathetic (rest-and-repair) mode during detox and recovery phases.

Why This Matters

  • Detox cannot occur in fight-or-flight
  • Reduces “chemical anxiety” and overstimulation
  • Improves sleep and digestion

Herbs Used

  • Lemon balm
  • Passionflower
  • Skullcap
  • Chamomile

Use approximately 1 teaspoon of each herb (or a combined pre-mixed blend).

Preparation & Use

  • Steep herbs in hot mineralized water (prepared with Pink Himalayan sole) for 10 minutes
  • Drink in the evening or during anxiety flares
  • Safe to use alongside binders and hydration

Especially helpful after parasite, metal, or EBV-focused days when the body feels “wired but tired.”
