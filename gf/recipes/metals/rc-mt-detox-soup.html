Green Support Soup

A warm, mineral-rich soup designed to support gentle drainage, lymph movement, and digestive ease — not aggressive detox.

This soup is meant to support the body, not force cleansing. It can be used during Foundations, detox phases, or whenever digestion feels sluggish.

Ingredients

  • Fresh cilantro (small handful)
  • Fresh parsley (small handful)
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 2–3 cups bone broth (or vegetable broth)
  • 1 clove garlic (optional, skip if sensitive)

Preparation

Simmer all ingredients gently until zucchini is soft. Blend if desired, or leave lightly chunky. Warm — do not boil aggressively.

Why This Helps

  • Provides minerals without overstimulation
  • Supports lymph and liver drainage
  • Easy to digest during detox phases
  • Pairs well with binders and hydration

When to Use

  • During parasite or metal support phases
  • When digestion feels heavy or slow
  • After sauna or sweating
  • As a light meal during low appetite days

This soup should feel grounding and nourishing. If symptoms increase, reduce herbs or pause.
