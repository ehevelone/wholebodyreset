Cilantro–Chlorella Detox Smoothie

A food-based metal support smoothie used selectively once hydration, minerals, and binders are already in place.

This smoothie is not a daily detox. It is an optional tool used to gently support metal mobilization with binding present.

Ingredients

  • 1 packed cup fresh cilantro
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup pineapple (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 teaspoon chlorella powder
  • Filtered water or coconut water (as needed)

Why This Works

  • Cilantro gently mobilizes stored metals
  • Chlorella provides binding support in the gut
  • Pineapple supports digestion and lymph flow
  • Banana provides potassium for nervous system balance

Mobilization without binding can worsen symptoms. This recipe intentionally pairs both.

When to Use

  • During metal support phases
  • After parasite cycles (with binders in place)
  • After sauna or sweating
  • During maintenance (1–2× per week)

Important Notes

  • Do not use daily long-term
  • Skip if anxiety, pressure, or nausea increases
  • Always maintain hydration and binders

If symptoms spike, pause and return to hydration, minerals, and gentle binders.
