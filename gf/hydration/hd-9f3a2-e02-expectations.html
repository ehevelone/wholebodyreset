Before You Go Further

By now, you should have at least spent some time with the Whole Body Reset – Foundations Edition.

You don’t need to have it memorized. You don’t need to understand everything yet. But having the context matters.

This program may feel slower than what you’re used to — and that’s intentional.

A few important expectations

  • This is not a detox sprint
  • More effort does not equal better results
  • Symptoms are feedback, not failure
  • Feeling worse is a sign to pause, not push

Guided Foundations works by rebuilding capacity first — hydration, mineral balance, and nervous system stability.

When those foundations are weak, even “gentle” protocols can feel overwhelming.

If at any point something feels like too much, the correct response is to slow down. There is no penalty for that here.

The next step is learning how to observe your body before making changes.
