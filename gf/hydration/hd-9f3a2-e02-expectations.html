Program Expectations

Before moving forward, it’s important to understand how Guided Foundations is designed to work.

This program follows a deliberate sequence. Each phase builds on the one before it, and the order matters.

This is a paced, structured program

Guided Foundations is not designed to be completed quickly. It is designed to be completed safely and sustainably.

Many people arrive here after trying to do too much, too fast — adding supplements, protocols, or detox strategies before their system was ready.

This program takes a different approach.

Doing less at first is intentional

At the beginning, very little is asked of you.

That is not because nothing is happening — it’s because stability comes before change.

Rushing ahead often creates new symptoms, confusion about what’s helping, or the feeling that nothing is working.

Slowing down prevents that.

Guidance is delivered gradually

You’ll receive guidance by email as you move through the program. This pacing is intentional.

It keeps your focus on the current step, prevents overload, and gives your body time to respond before anything new is introduced.

Skipping ahead usually creates more noise, not more progress.

Progress looks different for everyone

There is no universal timeline.

Some people notice subtle changes early. Others need more time for their system to settle. Both are normal.

The goal is not speed — it’s consistency, clarity, and tolerance.

Before anything is added

Before hydration guidance begins, there is one important step: learning how to observe your body without trying to fix or change it.

This baseline is what everything else depends on.
