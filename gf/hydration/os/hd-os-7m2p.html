Your Body Is Responding

This doesn’t mean you did anything wrong.

Some people notice that even gentle hydration changes can feel like “too much” at first — especially if the body has been depleted for a long time.

Common Signals at This Stage

  • Feeling wired or overstimulated
  • Head pressure or mild headaches
  • Restlessness or trouble sleeping
  • Increased bowel activity

These are not warning signs. They’re signs of activation.

The goal here is not to stop — it’s to slow.

Smaller amounts. More space between sips. Less intensity.

Your body is learning how to handle hydration again.

Helpful Hydration Options (Use Gently)

If hydration feels stimulating, these recipes allow you to adjust strength and pacing without stopping altogether.

  • Pink Himalayan Sole Water (Foundational)
  • Electrolyte Mineral Tea (Gentle Mineral Support)
  • Coconut Mineral Quencher (For Overstimulation or Sweating)

You can dilute further, reduce frequency, or pause briefly — hydration should feel supportive, not overwhelming.
