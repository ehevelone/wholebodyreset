Observation Comes First

Before adding anything — water, minerals, supplements — your first job is simply to notice.

Most of us are used to pushing through discomfort or trying to override symptoms. This program works differently.

Symptoms aren’t enemies here. They’re information.

What to pay attention to

  • Your energy throughout the day
  • How you sleep and how you wake up
  • Digestion and appetite
  • Thirst and fluid tolerance
  • Stress response and recovery

You’re not trying to fix any of this yet. You’re learning what “normal” looks like for you right now.

This baseline matters. It helps you recognize when something is helping — and when something is simply too much.
