Nervous System Regulation

This section of Guided Foundations is about safety, pacing, and how your body responds to change.

The nervous system governs how everything else in the body reacts — including hydration, minerals, digestion, sleep, and stress.

When the nervous system feels supported, progress often happens quietly. When it feels overwhelmed, even helpful inputs can feel like too much.

Before continuing, take a moment to notice:

  • Do things feel steadier than before?
  • Do they feel mostly the same?
  • Or do they feel more reactive or overwhelming?

There is no correct answer — only information.

The next pages are designed to meet you where your nervous system currently is.
