This Is a Safe Place to Pause

If your nervous system feels overwhelmed, there is no expectation to continue forward.

Overstimulation is not a personal failure. It is a signal that the system needs less input, not more effort.

At this stage, support may look like:

  • Holding steady without adding anything new
  • Reducing stimulation wherever possible
  • Allowing time for the nervous system to settle
  • Seeking individualized guidance if needed

Safety always comes before progress.

Many people choose to work alongside a:

  • Functional or integrative practitioner
  • Clinical herbalist
  • Licensed healthcare provider familiar with nervous system regulation

Guidance does not replace your intuition — it supports it.
