Your Nervous System May Be Overstimulated

This is more common than most people realize.

An overstimulated nervous system does not mean something is wrong. It means the body has received more input than it can comfortably process.

This can happen after stress, health challenges, lifestyle changes, or even well-intended interventions.

When the nervous system is overstimulated, the most supportive response is often less input — not more.

Safety comes before progress.

Guided Foundations treats overstimulation as a signal to pause, simplify, and allow the system to settle.

There is nothing you need to fix here. Awareness alone is the first step.
