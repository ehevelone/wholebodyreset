Parasites in Context

This section contains educational material from the Whole Body Reset – Foundations text. There is no requirement to take action here. Read, reflect, and apply only what feels supportive.

This Is Not a Diagnosis Section

Parasites can be part of the human ecosystem. Their presence does not automatically mean something is wrong, and it does not automatically require intervention.

This section exists to provide context — not urgency.

Why Parasites Are Discussed at All

Parasites are often blamed for symptoms that may stem from deeper imbalance — including mineral depletion, toxic load, nervous system stress, or impaired drainage.

Understanding parasites within a broader “terrain” view helps prevent fear-based decisions and unnecessary escalation.

No Action Is Required Here

Reading about parasites does not mean you need to treat, cleanse, or “go after” anything.

For many people, restoring hydration, minerals, sleep, and nervous system safety reduces symptoms that may not be parasite-driven in the first place.

Why Foundations Comes First

Parasite-focused work — when it is appropriate — is safest and most tolerable only after the body has sufficient support in place.

Foundations exists to ensure that any future steps, if taken at all, are gentle, informed, and non-reactive.

How to Use This Section

  • Read slowly
  • Notice what resonates — and what doesn’t
  • There is no need to label yourself
  • There is no timeline to follow

This information is here so you can make grounded decisions — not so you feel pressure to act.
