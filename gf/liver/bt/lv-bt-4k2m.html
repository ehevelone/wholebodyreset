Liver and lymph progress is often quiet. Improvement here may feel subtle rather than dramatic.

If you are here, your body may be responding by feeling slightly less burdened — not necessarily “better,” but steadier.

  • Less internal heaviness or pressure
  • Improved tolerance to hydration or minerals
  • More stable energy throughout the day
  • Slight improvement in digestion or sleep

These signs suggest circulation and drainage may be settling. They are not signals to change direction.

Consistency matters more than progression here.
