Simplifying the Approach

This page contains educational material from the Whole Body Reset – Foundations text. There is no requirement to implement anything here. Read and apply only what feels supportive.

When symptoms increase, the most supportive response is often to reduce internal stimulation.

Helpful adjustments may include:

  • Pausing herbal support
  • Returning to hydration and minerals only
  • Focusing on rest and gentle movement
  • Supporting lymph externally rather than internally

These changes are not setbacks. They help the body regain a sense of safety.

The goal is calm, not clearance.
