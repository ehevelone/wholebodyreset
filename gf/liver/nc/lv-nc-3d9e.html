When Patience Is the Work

This page contains educational material from the Whole Body Reset – Foundations text. There is no requirement to implement anything here. Read and apply only what feels supportive.

Liver and lymph systems often respond slowly, especially in people who have experienced past overload.

This does not mean:

  • You are doing something wrong
  • You need stronger interventions
  • Your body is failing to respond

Sometimes the most supportive step is allowing more time.

Staying steady is still a form of progress.
