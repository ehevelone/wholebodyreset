Kidney Support Protocols

This page is part of the Guided Foundations program. There is no requirement to implement anything here.

Purpose of Kidney Support

Kidney support is introduced to reduce overload, not to intensify detox.

Support may include:

  • Mineral-supported hydration
  • Reducing overall detox intensity
  • Gentle binders when tolerated
  • Warmth and rest

When to Slow Down

  • Rising anxiety or agitation
  • Sleep disruption
  • Head pressure or palpitations

Slowing down protects progress.
