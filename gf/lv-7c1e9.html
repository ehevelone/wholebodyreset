Liver & Lymph — Processing Capacity

The liver and lymph systems are responsible for processing internal load. When overall demand exceeds capacity, symptoms often intensify.

Before deeper load reduction is considered, hydration, minerals, digestion, and nervous system stability must be in place.

If processing feels overwhelming, the solution is typically more foundation — not more effort.
