Foundations Check — Drain & Stability

This page is a reference point within Whole Body Reset — Guided Foundations. It is not a gate, requirement, or test. Its purpose is awareness — nothing more.

Sometimes progress slows not because something is wrong, but because the body is still prioritizing protection over elimination.

This check exists to help you notice whether the basics feel supportive and stable before layering anything new.

  • Hydration feels supportive rather than bloating or draining
  • Minerals do not cause overstimulation or crashes
  • Bowel movements are regular and not forced
  • Lymph movement feels gentle, not exhausting
  • Stress response is quieter than before
  • Supports feel tolerated rather than overwhelming

If several of these feel like “not yet”, that does not indicate failure. It indicates useful information.

What This Usually Means

When foundations are still stabilizing, the body often benefits more from consistency than from additional protocols.

Drainage happens when safety is present — not when pressure is applied.

What This Page Is Not

  • Not a diagnosis
  • Not a delay tactic
  • Not a signal to stop your current phase
  • Not something you need to “pass”

Many people move forward successfully without ever returning to this page. It exists only as a stabilizing reference when needed.
