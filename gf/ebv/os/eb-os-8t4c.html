EBV — Nervous System Support

This page contains educational material from the Whole Body Reset – Foundations text. There is no requirement to implement anything here. Read and apply only what feels supportive.

EBV symptoms are often driven less by viral activity and more by nervous system overload. Supporting regulation is one of the most protective steps in this program.

Why Nervous System Support Matters

When the nervous system is overwhelmed, the body loses its ability to regulate immune activity, inflammation, sleep, and energy production.

In this state, even gentle supports can feel like “too much.”

Calm is not passive. Calm is what allows healing signals to be received.

Supportive Daily Practices

  • consistent sleep and wake times
  • gentle walking or light movement
  • deep breathing or slow exhalations
  • reducing sensory overload (screens, noise)
  • prioritizing rest over productivity

These practices may feel simple, but they are foundational to EBV stability.

What to Avoid During This Phase

  • pushing through fatigue
  • stacking new supplements
  • chasing symptoms aggressively
  • ignoring sleep disruption

Less input often creates more capacity.

Next Step
