EBV — Nervous System Support

Use this page when EBV symptoms feel louder because the system feels overloaded: wired, reactive, crash-prone, or unable to recover normally. Nothing here is meant to “push.” The goal is regulation and safety first.

In an overwhelmed state, the most protective move is often the simplest one: reduce stimulation, reduce pressure, and give the nervous system room to settle.

What This Page Is For

  • Lowering nervous system activation
  • Reducing reactivity and symptom spirals
  • Shortening crash time by protecting capacity
  • Helping the body regain predictable rhythms

Signs This Is the Right Page

  • Wired but exhausted
  • Sleep becomes lighter, fragmented, or non-restorative
  • Small stressors feel “too big”
  • Noise, screens, or conversation feel draining
  • Symptoms worsen when you try to “do more”

These are not failure signs. They are capacity signs.

Supportive Practices (Choose 1–2 Only)

Do not try to do everything at once. Choose one or two practices and repeat them consistently for several days.

  • Fixed sleep window: same bedtime and wake time (even if sleep is imperfect)
  • Light movement only: gentle walking or stretching that does not increase symptoms
  • Slow exhale breathing: longer exhale than inhale for 2–5 minutes
  • Reduce sensory input: lower screen time, dim lights, reduce noise
  • Rest before you crash: stop while you still feel “okay”

The goal is not motivation. The goal is downshifting. Consistency matters more than intensity.

What to Avoid Right Now

  • Pushing through fatigue
  • Testing your limits “to see if you can”
  • Stacking new supplements or strategies
  • Chasing symptoms with rapid changes
  • Ignoring sleep disruption and staying in high stimulation

Less input often creates more capacity.

How to Know You’re Improving

  • Symptoms stop escalating
  • Sleep becomes steadier (even slightly)
  • Recovery after activity shortens
  • Reactivity decreases (less “wired,” less sensitive)

Next Step

If you stabilize here, stay here. Do not advance just because you feel a little better. If symptoms continue to escalate or you feel unsafe in your body, move back to OS File 1 (stabilize) and/or use Support.
