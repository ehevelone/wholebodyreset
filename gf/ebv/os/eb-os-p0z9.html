EBV — Hold, Rest & Containment

This page contains educational material from the Whole Body Reset – Foundations text. There is no requirement to implement anything here. Read and apply only what feels supportive.

This page is a pause point.

If you are here, even gentle supports may feel like too much. The goal is not improvement — the goal is safety and stabilization.

Do not add new strategies.
Do not increase inputs.
Do not force progress.

EBV symptoms often intensify when the nervous system, immune system, or energy reserves are overwhelmed. Continuing to push during this phase can extend recovery time significantly.

What This Phase Focuses On

  • rest without guilt
  • simple hydration only, if tolerated
  • regular meals without experimentation
  • sleep protection
  • reducing physical and emotional demands

This is not inactivity. This is containment.

Holding steady preserves capacity. Capacity is what allows progress later.

When to Move Forward Again

Resume progression only when symptoms have clearly settled and daily rhythms feel predictable again. There is no timeline requirement.
