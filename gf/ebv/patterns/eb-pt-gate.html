EBV — Pattern Tier Gate

This page is a decision checkpoint. Nothing beyond this point is required. The purpose of this gate is to protect capacity, not to force progression.

EBV does not require constant action. In many cases, it responds best to restraint, consistency, and regulation.

Progress is determined by stability, not by how much is being done.

You May Proceed Past This Gate If:

  • Hydration is consistent and well tolerated
  • Minerals do not cause overstimulation
  • Binders are tolerated without crashes
  • Sleep is reasonably stable most nights
  • Emotional reactivity is decreasing
  • Fatigue is not progressively worsening

Stability matters more than symptom elimination.

You Should Remain Here If:

  • Symptoms escalate with added input
  • Anxiety or nervous system activation increases
  • Sleep becomes fragmented or unrefreshing
  • Detox reactions feel constant
  • Your system feels “on edge” most days

Remaining here is not regression. It is intelligent pacing.

EBV regulation improves when the system feels safe.

What This Gate Is Not

  • Not a diagnosis
  • Not a treatment plan
  • Not a requirement to escalate support

Many people see EBV patterns settle simply by strengthening foundations and avoiding unnecessary pressure.

Quiet stability is success.
