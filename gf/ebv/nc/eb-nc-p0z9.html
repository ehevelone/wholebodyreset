EBV — Hold & Stabilize

This page contains educational material from the Whole Body Reset – Foundations text. There is no requirement to implement anything here. Read and apply only what feels supportive.

This page is a pause point.

If you are here, the goal is not progress — the goal is stability.

Do not add new supports.
Do not escalate protocols.
Stay with what is currently tolerated.

EBV-related symptoms often intensify when the nervous system is pushed beyond its capacity. Holding steady allows regulation to return.

This phase protects against long crashes, prolonged flares, and setbacks that take weeks to unwind.

Holding is not failure. It is active protection.

When to Move Forward Again

Resume progression only when symptoms have clearly settled and daily rhythms feel predictable again.
