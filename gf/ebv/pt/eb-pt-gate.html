EBV — Pattern Tier Readiness Gate

This page is a pause and assessment point. Nothing beyond this gate is required. Its purpose is to protect pacing and capacity.

Epstein–Barr Virus does not behave the same way in every body. Symptoms and recovery timelines are shaped by terrain, nervous system regulation, and cumulative load — not viral presence alone.

The Pattern Tier is not meant to create urgency. It exists to provide context only if patterns are already present.

Before engaging with specific EBV pattern tracks, foundations should feel stable and supportive: hydration, minerals, sleep, nervous system regulation, and basic nourishment.

If any of those layers feel inconsistent or fragile, returning to them is the most productive step.

This gate exists to slow the process down, reduce fear-based decision making, and prevent escalation before capacity is ready.

Stability first. Information second.
