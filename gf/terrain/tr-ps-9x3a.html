Pause & Support

This page appears when the body signals that terrain support needs more time or gentler pacing.

Common signals may include:

  • Increased fatigue
  • Anxiety or nervous system agitation
  • Digestive slowing or constipation
  • Lymph congestion or pressure
  • Sleep disruption

These signals are not failure. They are communication.

What This Indicates

The body may be asking for:

  • Reduced stimulation
  • Slower pacing
  • More time with foundations
  • Individualized guidance if needed

Pausing protects the nervous system and prevents longer setbacks later.
